Fishing Squared

Game Instructions:

Decide who each player is and type their name into the respectiveplayer name boxes.

Your goal is to get the most points by fishing. Each player takes turn fishing by clicking on a square, with the winner being whoever has the most points when both players are out of casts.

There are two game modes, easy and difficult.
